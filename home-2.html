FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a drink minimum? +

Yes, we have a two-item minimum per person (can be food or drinks) to support our performers and venue.

What is the age requirement? +

Most shows are 21+ only. However, we occasionally host 18+ selective "All-Ages" afternoon showcases.

How do I perform at an Open Mic? +

Sign-ups for our Tuesday Open Mic start at 6:00 PM in person. The list fills up fast, so arrive early!
